How to Make Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • 200g digestive biscuits
    • Crushed
  • 100g butter, melted
  • 600g cream cheese
    • At room temperature
  • 100g powdered sugar
    • Sifted
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
    • Optional
      • Fresh fruit for topping
      • Fruit sauce
        • Strawberry
        • Blueberry
  • 284ml double cream
    • Whipped to soft peaks

Steps

  1. Crush the digestive biscuits and mix with melted butter. Press into the base of a springform tin.
  2. Chill the base in the fridge for 1 hour.
  3. Beat the cream cheese, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  4. Whip the double cream and fold into the cream cheese mixture.
  5. Spoon the mixture onto the biscuit base and smooth the top.
  6. Chill in the fridge for at least 4 hours or overnight.
    1. Optional: Top with fresh fruit or fruit sauce before serving.
  7. Remove from the tin and serve.
